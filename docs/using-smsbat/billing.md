# Billing & Payments

Here you can find information about payments, invoices, and balance management.

## How do I top up my balance?
Go to the Billing section in the dashboard, click "Add Funds", and select your preferred payment method.

## Where can I find my invoices?
Invoices are automatically generated at the beginning of each month and can be downloaded from the "Invoices" tab.

## What payment methods are supported?
We support Visa, Mastercard, and Wire Transfer.
